Students with weak prerequisites should avoid jumping directly into advanced JEE problems. First solve concept checks, then standard examples, then PYQs.

An error log should classify mistakes into concept gap, calculation slip, misread question, time pressure, and anxiety trigger. The next practice block should target the most frequent tag.

For topic revision, a useful loop is same-day recall, three-day review, and seven-day mixed practice. This works well for formulas, mechanisms, and standard problem types.
